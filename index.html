    • About me

    Composer for both instrumental and electroacoustic music, my intellectual interests lie at the crossroads of philosophy, music composition, analysis and computer music. I’m interested in formal approaches to music analysis (set-theory, transformational theory and formal methods) and their signifiance for the resolution of problems of composition, musical perception and computational representations. I’m also very engaged in the philosophical evaluation of analysis methods and composition techniques.
