    • About me

    Composer for both instrumental and electroacoustic music, my intellectual interests lie at the crossroads of philosophy, music composition, analysis and computer music. I’m interested in formal approaches to music analysis (set-theory, transformational theory and formal methods) and their signifiance for the resolution of problems of composition, musical perception and computational representations. I’m also very engaged in the philosophical evaluation of analysis methods and composition techniques.

    Education

    • Master’s Degree in Musical Creation and Sound Arts, INA-GRM and Gustave Eiffel University, 2025
    • D.E.M in Electroacoustic Composition, CRR Paris, 2025
    • Master’s Degree in Philosophy, Sorbonne University, 2024
    • Master’s Degree in German Studies, Sorbonne University, 2024
    • Bachelor in Philosophy, Sorbonne University, 2023
    • Bachelor in Sociology, Sorbonne University, 2023

    Skills

    Philosophy and sociology

    Advanced knowledges in history of philosophy, phenomenology and analytic philosophy. Quantitative sociology : agent-based modelling for social sciences, Statistics and probabilities, network analysis, cultural sociology.

    Music (theory and practice)

    Classical and jazz piano, tonal harmony, counterpoint, analysis. Theories and techniques of contemporary music, composition for instrumental and electroacoustic music, sound design, jazz arrangement.

    Computer music

    Digital Signal Processing, Digital Sound Synthesis, algorithms and computer music programming (C, Csound, SuperCollider, Max/MSP).

    DAW : Reaper, Pro Tools, Ableton Live.

    Experience

    Jazz piano

    Standard jazz, free jazz and experimental jazz (with musicians of Compagnie Lubat and conservatory of Bordeaux). Jazz fusion in Tom Ibarra Group. Concerts in Bordeaux, Paris, Angoulême, Toulouse.

    Classical piano

    Study of classical repertory, more specifically music of 19th century. Piano teacher in Bordeaux and currently in Paris. Concerts in Bordeaux with tenor singer and in violin-piano duo (conservatory of Bordeaux).

    Composition

    Jazz compositions for a jazz quartet of the American School of Modern Music, and for experimental jazz with a band of the conservatory of Bordeaux.

    Piano compositions in the style of early 20th century. Compositions for piano, trumpet, saxophone and doublebass in the style of Béla Bartók.

    Acousmatic compositions selected by the Groupe de Recherches Musicales for the master’s degree of musical creation and sound arts.

    Currently working on electroaoustic compositions for the Groupe de Recherches Musicales and the conservatory of Paris in the style of Musique Concrète and electronic serial music.

    Currently working on electroacoustic composition and sound design for an experimental animation film produced by Loqidor Production.
